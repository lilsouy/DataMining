Fun Fact: “Enta Omri” by Umm Kulthum
“Enta Omri” is an epic song that lasts for over 40 minutes! When it was first performed in 1964, it received such a warm reception that Umm Kulthum was asked to perform it four more times that same night.

Fun Fact: “Tamally Maak” by Amr Diab
“Tamally Maak” became an international hit, reaching the top of music charts not only in the Arab world but also in several European countries. The song’s music video, contributed to the song’s widespread popularity.
